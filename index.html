About AIgnite

Discover the community that's shaping the future of AI at IILM University

Igniting AI Innovation at IILM

AIgnite is the official Artificial Intelligence club of IILM University, Gurugram, established to create a community of AI enthusiasts who learn, build, and innovate together.

Our mission is to demystify AI and make it accessible to all students regardless of their background, while pushing the boundaries of what's possible with artificial intelligence.

We organize workshops, hackathons, and guest lectures from industry experts to provide hands-on experience with AI technologies and applications.
